🍃 Leaf 001 — The Lotus of Stillness

Ages 5–8 · Themes: stillness, patience, gentleness

Kid Story (read aloud)

In a quiet pond, a little seed rested under the water. It didn’t race. It didn’t shout. It just stayed calm.

Day by day, the seed grew roots where nobody could see. Then one morning, a beautiful lotus flower opened on the surface. It wasn’t magic from rushing. It was magic from waiting.

The pond whispered: “Stillness helps good things grow. Patience is not doing nothing. Patience is growing quietly—until you’re ready.”

What it teaches

Stillness can be a kind of strength.

Sometimes the most important growing happens where we can’t see it yet.

Word Pond (3 words)

stillness = quiet on purpose patience = waiting without getting mean roots = the hidden part that holds you strong

Ripple Question

When do you feel most calm—like the pond?

Play Together (2–5 minutes)

The Pond Listening Game

  1. Sit quietly for 30 seconds.
  2. Each person says 3 sounds they heard.
  3. Say together: “We can be still. We can grow.”
